Chicago Hyperloop

Speeding into the Future

We are a UIC student lead organization who build and race hyperloop pods. We hope to eventually help the world switch to a more clean, faster. safer and reliable mode of transportation. Hyperloop pods run in a vacuum tube, and levitate
Nomatter who you are, we have a place for you on our team, and we are always looking for new members. from design and marketing majors to science and engineering majors, we are always looking for people to join our team. learn hands on practical knowledge, from soldering, 3d printing, leadership, physics, raspberry pi and arduino, to web development and more.
Join our team of people and eat instant Ramen with us, join our discord or email chicagohyperloop@gmail.com and join one of our meetings at the UIC makerspace. for more information, show up to one of our meetings at the UIC makerspace
