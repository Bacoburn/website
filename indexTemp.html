Chicago Hyperloop Speeding into the Future
Bringing Better Futures faster
We are a UIC student lead organization who build and race hyperloop pods, to eventually help the world switch to a more clean, faster. safer and reliable mode of transportation.
Hyperloop, like a maglev train, levitates and uses electromagnets for propulsion, but unlike maglev trains they operate in a vacuum tube to reduce friction, and they operate in smaller "pods" to ensure the most efficient use of the transport device-width
