We are Chicago Hyperloop, a UIC student organization that builds and races hyperloop pods hyperloop is a transportation mehanism similar to a train It uses special Linear induction Motors to levitate off the track and propel itself to ~600mph The track itself is a tube

Join us in building the future of transportation! We have projects, competitions, leadership roles and opportunities for undergraduate students, graduate students, and Alumni.

We have positions related to:

  • UIC College of engineering majors
  • UIC College of Business Administration
  • Math and Science majors
  • Design majors


We will take all majors and backgrounds, from beginer to experienced. We are chill, we are fun, we are friendly. We are all about making the world a better place.

We meet online and in person at the makerspace
