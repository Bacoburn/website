About


Hyperloop is essentially a high speed train that levitates and goes 600 mph in a vacuum tube.
We have many teams including:
  • The Controls and Propulsion Team
    • -Uses arduinos, raspberry pi's, electrical circuits and software to build our prototype
  • The Mechanical Team
    • -Designs and assembles our protoype using CAD, mechanical components, and various machining tools
  • Linear Induction Motor Team
    • -Uses magnetic simulations to design the linear induction motor which creates levitation and thrust
  • The Marketing Team
    • -Manages budget, resources, social media and advertising
