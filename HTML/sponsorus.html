What We Need From You


Building a pod isn't easy, so there are many ways you can help us! This ranges from monetary donations to supplying us with specific materials and parts, such as aluminum, carbon-fiber, laminated electrical steel for motors, various tools and hardware, transportation, and lodging for when we compete in the Canadian Hyperloop Conference. We are also looking for sponsors to help us with our events and outreach.

We are always looking for new sponsors and partners. For further sponsorship information, please reach out to us at chicagohyperloop@gmail.com.

What We'll Do For You


We will be sure to promote your company and brand through our social media and website, and we will also be sure to discuss you at our recruitment events. Furthermore, your contribution will be reflected in our pod design. Your company logo will be displayed on the front of our pod when we compete in the Canadian Hyperloop Conference, available for all to see.
As you can see from the picture we have plenty of space on the pods Carbon Fiber Shell, we hope you can help change that.
All Benefits will last at least 3 years.

Tier 2: $500 - $1000 8in x 8in logo on Pod, put logo on the Merch Plus Tier 1 Benefits
Tier H: $1000+ Invitation to competitions, and special mentions at Events and where applicable, Plus Tier 2 Benefits
